GRAFIGHT - NO MORE MAINTENANCE FRIGHT!

“Looking up into the night sky is looking into infinity – distance is incomprehensible and therefore meaningless” The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy

For corporations that are users of unique, custom-made software solutions, the phenomenon of software-erosion causes a significant problem.

This process starts during software development and deepens with the addition of new logic and increased business processes. Companies with large teams of developers are also struggling with this problem that can be magnified ten-fold in companies without a private IT sector. Software erosion means that the quality of a software deteriorates continuously during its life cycle.

Typical projects use narrow time frames, and under the pressure of deadlines, developers have to make compromises. Later on, the client may expand the software with new functions or try to correct the errors, but most likely confronted by the problem that the code is hardly readable, and the need may arise to translate the code again. Moreover, dimensions of a software-code are typically huge, and for a third party almost impossible to understand.

It may also happen that the original company developing the code has since gone out of business (or ceased relationship with the partners), then the product must be given to another IT company for the time-consuming process of having to recognise and understand the third-party software-code which then results in enhanced costs of development growth. Our service can help avoid these problems.

After checking the code, we make recommendations about the choices of code-fixing with a keen eye on keeping costs low. The Code-Refactoring is part of our Research and Development project with Szeged University. Our product is based on the latest results in software-erosion research. This tool dramatically enhances code reliability for the long haul.

Companies running custom-made computer programs cannot afford to overlook our code-refactoring software.

Steps typically include:

  1. We sign an agreement and an NDA. Since this is a sensitive field, we must clarify all the legal issues. We handle all information carefully and discretely.
  2. Our partner gives us access to the code base and we send it through our system. After code evaluation we produce a ranking table. This takes 2-4 days.
  3. Guided by the ranking table we evaluate all aspects of code improvements and write a document with our recommendations on what, where and how should be changed. The changes can be done either by the original developer company or another one.
    This step typically takes 1-2 months depending on the size of code and the depth of the recommended changes.
  4. If desired, we can re-develop the system based on our own recommendations.

Why GRAfight:

  • Improved code base readability for companies
  • Reduction in issue management and hotfix turn-around times
  • Hidden issues and bugs are found during the coding phase and not post release
  • Faster and safer software availability and releases
  • Faster but less frequent release schedules as the software is proven fully operational, not just released
  • Target group: large companies that develop their own complex systems
