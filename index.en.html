GRAFIGHT - NO MORE MAINTENANCE FRIGHT!

“Looking up into the night sky is looking into infinity – distance is incomprehensible and therefore meaningless” The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy

For those corporations who are users of unique developed software, the phenomenon of software-erosion cause significant problem.

This process starts during software developing and deepens with adding new functions and acting up. Those companies are also struggle against this phenomenon, where there are employed highly qualified developers. However, where there is no private IT sector, this problem cause serious consequences exponentially. The software-erosion means that the quality of software is deteriorating continuously during its life cycle.

Typical developing projects are in a narrow time frame, and under the pressure of deadlines, developers are tend to make compromises. Later, the client would expand its software with new function or try to correct errors, then he confronts that problem, the code is hardly readable, and they have to translate software again. Moreover, dimensions of a software-code are inconceivably big, and almost impossible to understand.

It may happen, that the company which have developed the original software had been disappeared (or ceased the relation between the partners), then the product must be given to another IT company, but it can cause serious problems, because they had to recognise and understand the software-code, thereby the costs of development grow, and it takes more time. Our service can help to avoid these situations.

After checking the code, we make a suggestion about changing methods, and how they can keep low costs. The Code-Refactoring is part of our project of Research and Development, co-operated with Szeged University. The product based on the newest research results and the aim is to protect against the software-erosion. This tool can grow the reliability in the long run.

Typical steps in a project:

  1. We sign an agreement and an NDA. This is a sensitive field so we should clarify all the legal issues.
    We handle the information carefully and discretely.
  2. Our partner let us access the code base and we send it through our system.
    After this evaluation we get a table with ranking. This takes 2-4 days.
  3. Based on the ranking we evaluate the different aspects and write a document with our recommendations where, what and how should be changed. The changes can be done either by the original developer company or another one.
    This step lasts for 1-2 months (based on the size).
  4. If you order we can re-develop the system based on our own recommendations.

Why GRAFIGHT?

  • Improved code base readability for companies
  • Faster and safer software availability and releases
  • Reduction in issue management and hotfix turn-around times
  • Hidden issues and bugs are found during the coding phase and not post release
  • Faster but less frequent release schedules as software works, not just released
  • Target group: large companies that develop their own complex systems
